Darkmode.JS · Add a dark-mode / night-mode to your website in a few seconds.

This library uses the css mix-blend-mode in order to bring Dark-mode to any of your websites. Just copy paste the snippet and you will get a widget to turn on and off the dark-mode.

You can also use it without the widget programmatically. The plugin is lightweight, built in VanillaJS. It also uses localstorage by default so your last setting will be remembered !

You can see on the lower right side, a circle taht when you toggle, will swtich the page to dark/white mode.
